## Overview

The creditor card is the atom of the Americor app. Everything users care about — settled debts, money saved, what's next — comes through it. Designing it meant designing the whole program experience in miniature: 11 states the card can be in, 5 transitions between them, and a motion language that explains where you are without pulling focus.

## What I owned

- State catalogue: enrollment → savings → negotiation → offer → settled → paid-off, plus error / on-hold / cancelled / re-engaged variants
- Transition motion: subtle wipes for status, full bloom for celebrations, no animation at all when the user is mid-task
- Empty states across the lifecycle — first-day, mid-program, post-settlement
- Density rules: which numbers go on the card, which go in the detail sheet, which never show

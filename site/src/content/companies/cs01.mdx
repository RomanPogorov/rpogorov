## Overview

Americor — debt-settlement fintech. Three platforms (web, iOS, Android), one shared design language, two consumer brands, a settlement process that hadn't been mapped end-to-end. The brief wasn't "modernise the look" — it was "make this scale without melting every time we add a brand or feature."

## What I owned

- Three-platform audit (web, iOS, Android) — every screen catalogued and clustered
- 4-layer token architecture (root → semantic → brand → platform) — unified theming, brand-switchable via Figma modes
- Full design system rebuild in Figma + Code Connect to React + native
- IPO-cycle redesign of consumer-facing flows
- Code-first prototypes for the riskier patterns (community feed, NSF simulator)

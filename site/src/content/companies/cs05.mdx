## Overview

Consulting engagement: Databricks asked for help with the gap between newcomers (who get crushed by the depth of the platform) and power users (who want every control surfaced). The thesis was simple — reorganise nav around tasks, not modules — but the platform's surface area meant every change had to clear three engineering teams.

## What I owned

- Nav restructuring: from "where engineering puts the code" to "what users come here to do"
- Density rules — when to surface, when to nest, when to deep-link out to docs instead of cluttering the workspace
- Onboarding flow that treats power-user features as opt-in surfaces rather than hidden ones
- Data-viz audit: defaults for the most common chart types, escape hatches for the long tail

## Outcome

Recommendations went to the platform team. Not all shipped — a consulting role is about leaving the right map, not the keys.

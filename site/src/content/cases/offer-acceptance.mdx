## Background & problem

Americor is a debt-settlement company that negotiates users' debts to lower them. After negotiating a settlement, it's presented to a user for acceptance. **Americor only collects fees and gets revenue once an offer is accepted.**

<div class="row-2">
  <PainCard tag="biz" title="Business problem">
    The majority of settlements were accepted through phone calls — high-cost, resource-heavy, and a clear lack of self-service.
  </PainCard>
  <PainCard tag="user" title="User pain">
    Users were reluctant to accept settlements online — gaps in understanding, motivation, and communication across touchpoints, worsened by dreary patterns.
  </PainCard>
</div>

## Solution breakdown

Drive online acceptance through clarity, urgency and a more appealing experience across multiple touchpoints.

<div class="row-3">
  <Pillar num="01" label="appeal">A more appealing UI to excite and engage users — removing easily-dismissed and disruptive patterns.</Pillar>
  <Pillar num="02" label="clarity">Ensuring clarity and minimising misconceptions through rejection guidance — enabling informed decisions.</Pillar>
  <Pillar num="03" label="urgency">Creating urgency and reminders through fixed banners visible on every screen.</Pillar>
</div>
